Site purpose

The purpose of this website is to display weather in a clear and professional way

Target Audience

gwweather.com will be Practical minded people that want weather wihtout fluff. Age range from 20-40 young business/Students Who want weather presented neatly and at a glance. This site will be designed around the fact that most in demographic Use cell phones but sometimes use online web-browsers

Website is also targeting the older demographic with. For people that have hobbies that may want to get a quick glance at the weather. They want to beable to check the weather, that said this demographic will be checking likely more on a computer. That in mind the website my be slightly altered for this demographic to include larger fonts and ensure colors with enough Contrast

Scenario

Looking to get a quick look of weather, will be posted large and upfront

Wants to check weather at home and at fishing lake

Wants to check weather only at destination

Wants to check the weather at a family members location

Quick simple access to clear data

Color Scheme

Color scheme main 000040, with a secondary of 404000, offset by C0C000 Text will be white.

typography

Main font will be font-family: 'Fjalla One', sans-serif; as is very easy to read without bolding. Roboto maybe used in less important aspects of the page as well as open sans, colors will be either white or dark blue as defined in color scheme
